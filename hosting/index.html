Privacy Policy

Your Bridge School
Last updated: June 15, 2026
Contact: contact@yourbridgeschool.com

Your Bridge School ("we," "our," or "us") operates the mobile application Your Bridge School (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information when you register or use the App:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Profile photo
  • National ID number (for certificate issuance)
  • Payment records (amount, method, sessions paid — managed offline by the school)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, OS version, device identifiers)
  • App usage data and crash reports
  • Push notification tokens

2. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you: when you register, fill out forms, upload files, or contact us
  • Through third-party login providers: Google and Facebook (with your consent)
  • Automatically: through Firebase services and app analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide educational services, course content, and class management
  • Track attendance, progress reports, and certificates
  • Facilitate communication between learners, teachers, and administrators
  • Process and record payments (cash, card, or transfer)
  • Send push notifications for updates and reminders
  • Improve the App through crash reporting and usage analysis
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Firebase (by Google)

  • Firebase Authentication — for email/password, Google, and Facebook login
  • Firebase Realtime Database — to store app data (user profiles, courses, payments, messages, etc.)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — to send push notifications
  • Firebase Crashlytics — for crash reporting and app stability

Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google Sign-In

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Facebook Login

Facebook Data Policy: https://www.facebook.com/about/privacy

5. Payments

The App does not process online payments directly. All payments (cash, card, or bank transfer) are handled manually by the school administration and recorded in the App for ledger and subscription management purposes. We do not collect or store full credit card numbers or banking details. Payment method information (e.g., "Cash", "Card", "Transfer") is stored only for internal record-keeping.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data:

  • With teachers and administrators within the school for educational purposes
  • With service providers (Firebase/Google) who process data on our behalf
  • When required by law or to protect legal rights
  • With your consent

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion or when no longer required, data will be deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law (e.g., financial records).

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including Firebase's built-in security rules, encrypted communications (HTTPS), and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@yourbridgeschool.com.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is used in an educational context and may involve data from children under 13 (or the applicable age of consent). We collect this data only with parental or school consent and only for educational purposes. If you believe a child has provided data without consent, please contact us to have it removed.

11. Third-Party Login

When you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive only the information you authorize (e.g., name, email, profile picture). We do not access your social media posts, friends list, or other non-authorized data.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
